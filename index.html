About This Project

This comprehensive Tableau-based data analytics project analyzes college students' food choices, dietary habits, nutrition patterns, and health-related factors. The interactive dashboards and visualizations provide insights into eating behaviors, nutritional intake, and overall health indicators.

Explore multiple perspectives including comfort food reasons, healthy food consumption, exercise and weight relationships, and more.
